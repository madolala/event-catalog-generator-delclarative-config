The Payment Team is responsible for the development, maintenance, and optimization of all payment processing systems and integrations. This includes handling payment transactions, managing payment gateways, ensuring PCI compliance, and providing a seamless payment experience for customers across all platforms.

## Responsibilities

### 1. Payment Processing & Integration
- **Payment Gateways**: Integrating and maintaining connections with various payment gateways and providers.
- **Transaction Management**: Processing and managing payment transactions securely and efficiently.
- **Payment Methods**: Supporting multiple payment methods (credit cards, digital wallets, bank transfers, etc.).
- **Currency Support**: Handling multi-currency transactions and currency conversion.

### 2. Payment Security & Compliance
- **PCI Compliance**: Ensuring all payment systems comply with PCI-DSS standards.
- **Fraud Prevention**: Implementing fraud detection and prevention mechanisms.
- **Secure Data Handling**: Ensuring secure handling and storage of payment information.
- **Audit & Monitoring**: Monitoring payment transactions and maintaining audit trails.

### 3. Payment Experience & Optimization
- **Checkout Optimization**: Optimizing the checkout and payment flow for better conversion rates.
- **Payment Reconciliation**: Managing payment reconciliation and settlement processes.
- **Error Handling**: Implementing robust error handling and retry mechanisms for failed payments.
- **Refunds & Chargebacks**: Managing refund processing and chargeback handling.
