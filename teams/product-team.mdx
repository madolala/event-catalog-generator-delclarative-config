The Product Team is responsible for defining and driving the product vision, strategy, and roadmap for all company offerings. This includes conducting market research, gathering customer feedback, prioritizing features, and ensuring that the products deliver value to customers while meeting business objectives.

## Responsibilities

### 1. Product Strategy & Vision
- **Product Roadmap**: Defining and maintaining the product roadmap aligned with business goals.
- **Market Research**: Conducting market research and competitive analysis to identify opportunities.
- **Product Vision**: Establishing and communicating the product vision across the organization.
- **Stakeholder Management**: Collaborating with stakeholders to align on product priorities and objectives.

### 2. Product Development & Delivery
- **Feature Prioritization**: Prioritizing features and enhancements based on customer needs and business value.
- **Requirements Definition**: Writing detailed product requirements and user stories for development teams.
- **User Experience**: Working with design teams to ensure optimal user experience and usability.
- **Release Planning**: Planning and coordinating product releases and go-to-market strategies.

### 3. Product Analytics & Optimization
- **Metrics & KPIs**: Defining and tracking key product metrics and performance indicators.
- **User Feedback**: Gathering and analyzing customer feedback to drive product improvements.
- **A/B Testing**: Designing and running experiments to optimize product features and user flows.
- **Product Analytics**: Using data and analytics to make informed product decisions and identify growth opportunities.
